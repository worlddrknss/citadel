# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in the Citadel project should be respectful, technical, and constructive.

Expected behavior:

- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements in terms of code, behavior, and evidence.
- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project spaces.
- Accept feedback and change direction when new facts warrant it.
- Keep security-sensitive details private until maintainers agree they can be disclosed.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith review behavior.
- Publishing private or security-sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, reject contributions, or remove access for behavior that undermines a safe and productive working environment.

## Reporting conduct issues

Report conduct issues to the maintainers through the repository owner's published contact channels. Avoid public escalation when private moderation is practical.
